# TradeUp Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 2026**

## Introduction

TradeUp by Cardflow Labs ("we", "our", or "the App") is a Shopify application that provides loyalty program and trade-in management services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### From Merchants (Store Owners)
- Shopify store information (store name, domain)
- Contact information (email address)
- Billing information (processed by Shopify)

### From Customers (Store Visitors)
- Name and email address (when joining loyalty program)
- Phone number (optional)
- Purchase history (synced from Shopify)
- Loyalty program data (tier status, points balance, store credit)
- Trade-in history and records

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide loyalty program functionality
- Track and manage store credit balances
- Process trade-in transactions
- Send transactional emails (balance updates, tier changes)
- Improve our services

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely on encrypted servers
- We use industry-standard security measures
- Data is hosted in the United States
- We do not sell personal information to third parties

## Data Sharing

We only share data with:
- **Shopify**: To integrate with your store and process payments
- **Service Providers**: Hosting and infrastructure (Railway, PostgreSQL)

We do NOT share data with advertisers or sell customer lists.

## Data Retention

- Merchant data: Retained while app is installed, deleted upon uninstallation request
- Customer data: Retained as long as merchant account is active
- Trade-in records: Retained for accounting purposes per merchant settings

## Your Rights

### Merchants
- Export all customer data via the app dashboard
- Delete customer records individually or in bulk
- Uninstall the app to cease data collection

### Customers
- Request access to your loyalty data through the store
- Request deletion by contacting the store owner

## GDPR Compliance

For EU customers:
- We process data based on legitimate interest and consent
- You may request data access, correction, or deletion
- Contact the store owner or us directly for requests

## Children's Privacy

TradeUp is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email to merchants.
